Use this page to understand when to accept findings and how to complete the acceptance workflow.

## Understand what acceptance means

Accepting a finding records that the risk is known and temporarily tolerated. It helps teams focus on active remediation work without losing visibility into known exceptions.

Acceptance is not a fix. To reduce risk, you must remediate the underlying condition.

## Decide when to accept or remediate

Use **acceptance** when:

* You have reviewed the risk and agreed to tolerate it for a defined period.
* You are waiting for a change to complete, such as a retention window.

Use **remediation** when:

* You are ready to remove the risky condition.
* You want posture trends to reflect actual risk reduction.

For broader guidance on findings, see [Understand Findings and Remediation](/analyze-data/findings/attack-paths).

## Know what changes in the product

When you accept a finding principal, it is hidden from the default principal table view for that finding.

The principal and related edges still appear in other analysis views, including **Explore** and **Posture**.

## Before you begin

* Sign in with a role that can accept attack path impacted principals. For role details, see [Administering users and roles](/manage-bloodhound/auth/users-and-roles).

## Accept a finding principal

<Steps>
  1. Navigate to the **Attack Paths** page.
  2. Expand the finding, open the menu to the left of the principal name (three vertical dots), and select **Accept**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/assets/image1-19.png" />
  </Frame>

  3. In the **Accept Attack Path** window, set the number of days for acceptance and select **Accept**.

     If you are accepting while waiting for a change to leave retention, set the duration based on that scenario. For example, for **Logons from Tier Zero Users**, set the duration to 7 days. For more details, see [Data reconciliation and retention in BloodHound Enterprise](/collect-data/enterprise-collection/data-retention).

  <Frame>
    <img src="/assets/image1-20.png" />
  </Frame>
</Steps>

## Remove acceptance from a finding principal

<Steps>
  1. Navigate to the **Attack Paths** page.
  2. Expand the finding and enable the **Accepted** toggle.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/assets/image1-21.png" />
  </Frame>

  3. Open the menu to the left of the accepted principal (three vertical dots), and select **Remove Acceptance**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/assets/image1-22.png" />
  </Frame>

  4. In the **Remove Attack Path Acceptance** window, select **Remove Acceptance**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/assets/image1-23.png" />
  </Frame>
</Steps>

## Outcome

After acceptance, the principal is hidden from the default principal table for that finding until you enable the **Accepted** toggle. The principal and related edges remain visible in **Explore** and **Posture**.
